Health Tips Based on BMI

Underweight

  • 🏋️ Focus on strength training to build muscle mass.
  • 🍽️ Eat calorie-dense foods like nuts, dairy, eggs, and avocados.
  • 🧘‍♂️ Include yoga or pilates to improve posture and body control.
  • 🛌 Get at least 7-9 hours of sleep for muscle recovery.

Suggested Workout Plan:
- 3x/week full-body strength workouts (e.g., squats, push-ups, rows)
- 2x/week light cardio (15-20 mins walking or cycling)
- 2x/week flexibility or yoga

Fit

  • 💪 Maintain a balanced mix of strength and cardio exercises.
  • 🥦 Continue eating a varied and nutrient-rich diet.
  • 🚶‍♂️ Aim for 7,000–10,000 steps a day.
  • 🧘 Stay consistent with weekly movement and manage stress.

Suggested Workout Plan:
- 3x/week strength training (upper/lower split)
- 3x/week moderate-intensity cardio (jog, bike, swim)
- 1 day active rest (walk, yoga, stretching)

Obese

  • 🚶‍♂️ Begin with low-impact exercises (walking, elliptical).
  • 🥗 Focus on whole foods, reduce sugary & processed items.
  • 💧 Stay hydrated and avoid liquid calories (soda, sweet drinks).
  • 🧠 Consider working with a trainer and dietitian for guidance.

Suggested Workout Plan:
- 5x/week low-impact cardio (30–45 mins walk, swim)
- 2x/week bodyweight strength training (wall sits, knee push-ups)
- Daily stretching for mobility
